Indian Coast Guard Exams​

Indian Coast Guard Exam Details

1. Exam Pattern

Candidates applying for the Indian Coast Guard Posts must go through the eligibility criteria before filling the Indian Coast Guard application form. Candidates must be 10th, +2, Degree & Diploma passed from a recognized institution with Maths and Physics as their subjects. Must also have scored at least an aggregate mark of 50% in class 10. Besides that, a relaxation in the percentage aggregate of 5% will be given for SC/ ST candidates and for outstanding sportspersons.

Note: the candidates who have passed from ‘Open Schools’ not recognized by the government/ AICTE are not eligible.

Note: physically challenged candidates are not eligible for the post.

Eligibility Criteria

Category Educational Qualification Age Limit
Officers Degree Pass 18 to 22 years
Navik (GD) 10th Pass 18 to 22 years
Navik (DB) 10th & +2 Pass 18 to 22 years
Yantrik Technical Diploma Pass 18 to 22 years

2. Written Examination

A written exam will be conducted for the post of Officers, Navik (GD & DB) and , Yantrik Technical which consist of questions which are objective in nature. The Indian Coast Guard Syllabus consists of the following subjects as listed below.

  • General Sciences
  • Mathematics
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • General English
  • General Awareness (Current Affairs & GK)
  • Reasoning (Verbal & Non-verbal)

4. Selection Process

  • The applications received for the posts will be scrutinized and only eligible and appropriate applications will be accepted. The officials will also mention the reason for the rejection of the application form if the application is rejected.
  • In the next step the e- admit card will be released. Candidates will have to carry all the required documents to the exam centre for verification. Only valid applications will be considered for further processing
  • Candidates will then have to attend the written test and candidates clearing the written test will be called for Physical Fitness Test.
  • Candidates clearing both these tests will go through a medical examination to get shortlisted for the training.
  • However, candidates failing to complete the training will not be considered for further selection. Successful trainees will then be included in the final merit list.

5. Documents Required at the Exam Centre

Candidates will have to carry these documents along with the Indian Coast Guard admit card . These documents are to be submitted at the examination centre. Candidates will have to carry both original and attested photocopies of the certificates. The original documents will be returned after verification.

  • Three copies of the pre-filled e-admit card.
  • Two copies of the application form.
  • Original and photocopy of class 10 pass certificate/mark sheet.
  • Ten passport-sized photographs (blue background).
  • Valid Photo ID Proof (Driving License, Voter ID, Aadhaar, Passport, etc.).
  • Original caste certificate (if applicable).
  • Sports certificate (if applicable).
